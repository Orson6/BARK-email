Here's why BARK exists.

Back in 2014, dogs all around me, my friends' dogs, my family's dogs, kept getting ill. Itchy skin. Upset tummies. Allergies nobody could pin down. So I went looking. I found raw feeding, and I loved it. But I also knew most owners can't realistically live the freezer-and-defrosting life every single day.

Nobody was making dry food that came close to a proper raw diet. So I made one.

BARK takes the popular 80:20 raw recipe, dehydrates real UK-sourced ingredients, and gently cold-presses them at 45°C. No fillers. No grains. No nutrient-killing high heat. Just raw-quality nutrition you can keep in the cupboard.
